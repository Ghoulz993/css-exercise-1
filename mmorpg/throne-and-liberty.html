Throne and Liberty

Throne and Liberty is an upcoming MMORPG developed by NCSOFT. Set in a vast open world, the game promises to deliver a rich narrative experience combined with dynamic gameplay mechanics. Players will be able to explore diverse environments, engage in large-scale battles, and interact with a living ecosystem.

The game features a class system that allows players to customize their characters and playstyles. With a focus on cooperative gameplay, players can team up to tackle challenging content and participate in large-scale PvP battles. Throne and Liberty aims to create a seamless and immersive experience, drawing players into its expansive world.
