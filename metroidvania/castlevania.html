Video Game Genres

Castlevania

This is a page about Castlevania, a popular Metroidvania game series developed by Konami. The series is known for its gothic horror themes, intricate level design, and challenging gameplay. Players typically control a member of the Belmont family as they battle against Dracula and his minions.

The gameplay often involves exploration, puzzle-solving, and combat, with players gaining new abilities and weapons as they progress. The series has been influential in the development of the Metroidvania genre, inspiring many other games with its blend of action, exploration, and storytelling.
